Steps for Tata Sky Pack Modification

Click on My Account

Click on Login

Enter Mobile Number/ Subscriber ID

Click on Continue

Click on Menu

Click on Manage Packs

Enter account password/Click on Login using OTP through mobile number

Enter OTP

CLick on Submit

Click on Secondary Box

Click on Modify Pack for changes in Secondary Box

Click on Cross Icon

Click on Primary Box

Click on Modify Pack for changes in Primary Box

Click on Channels

Choose Channels from the list

Click on Select and Proceed

Click on Confirm

Successfully Changed , Click on Back to selfcare
